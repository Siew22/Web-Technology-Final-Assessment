Our Background

Mission: Dedicated to wildlife conservation, education, and providing a sanctuary for endangered species in Borneo and beyond.

Highlights: Home to over 200 species of animals! Experience our interactive feeding sessions, explore the tropical bird aviary, and learn from our dedicated zookeepers. Sibu Zoo is the perfect getaway for nature lovers and families.
